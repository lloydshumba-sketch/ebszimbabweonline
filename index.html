Admissions Questions

Clear answers before you commit.

A premium school reduces uncertainty. Students should understand how classes, recordings, assignments and support work before committing.

Are classes suitable for working professionals?

Yes. The learning model is designed around evening-friendly live sessions, recorded access and structured support for students balancing work and study.

Do students receive lecture recordings?

Yes. Recorded lecture access supports revision, catch-up and repeated review of complex topics.

Are assignments marked?

Yes. Marked practice is part of the learning experience because students need to know exactly where marks are being lost.

How do students ask questions?

Students may use live class interaction, WhatsApp support and direct guidance channels depending on the tuition arrangement.

Can I start before the next exam cycle?

Yes. The early start route is recommended for students who want more time for technical understanding, question practice and revision discipline.
